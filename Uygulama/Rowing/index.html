The 2025 European Rowing Under 19 Championships have been attributed to Kruszwica, Poland. The competition dates are 24 -25 May 2025. The 2025 European Rowing Coastal and Beach Sprint Championships are provisionally attributed to Manavgat, Antalya, Türkiye from 9 – 12 October 2025. Beach Sprint teams should remain available on 13 October for a spare day of racing. The competition venue is Sorgun Çamiçi Public Beach, Manavgat. The provisional attribution is pending the submission of outstanding bid documents.
